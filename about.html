About Us

Quality is our mission

Our chefs bring years of culture and tradition to the dinner table with high-end Chinese cuisine.
Our vision for the future is to create experiential dining that is more than just a night out. We aim to bring quality and luxury across all aspects of our brand. Within our vision always lives the promise of inspiring creativity, conversation and quality.

Hand prepared with fresh ingredients

Our ingrediants are fresh and all of our vegetables are sourced locally becuase we understand the importance of supporting local farms and vendors. Fresh ingredients enhance the flavor of each dish, prepared by chefs who know the imapact that a hand prepared meal has on the dining experience.
